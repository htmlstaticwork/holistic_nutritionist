Questions & Answers

Do you take insurance?

Currently, we provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement, depending on your out-of-network benefits.

Can I work with you remotely?

Yes! 100% of our coaching and programs are delivered digitally via secure video and chat systems, allowing us to support clients globally.

Is this a medical cure?

No. We offer nutritional and lifestyle support to manage health and vitality. We do not claim to cure medical conditions and always recommend consulting your primary physician.
